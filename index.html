Step 1: The 3-Layer Rule

Every winter outfit = Base + Middle + Outer

1. Base Layer

Heat Trap

Thin but warm, right on the skin.

  • Thermal long-sleeve tops
  • Thermal leggings
  • Fleece-lined leggings (for extra cozy days)

2. Middle Layer

Insulation

The "cozy" layer that holds warmth in.

  • Hoodies
  • Fleece zip-ups
  • Sweaters

3. Outer Layer

Weather Shield

Blocks wind, snow, rain.

  • Puffer coat or parka
  • Snow pants (very cold / snow)
  • Optional: lightweight rain/shell jacket

Then add: hat + neck gaiter + waterproof gloves + warm socks + winter boots.
